Rio Grande Mexican Restaurant Logo Redesign

A new logo for the Rio Grande Mexican Restaurant that would work across different types of media.

Instead of a total redesign of the Rio's iconic logo I recommended simplification instead. The final result was this beautifully simplified, legible version that would work on print and digital while preserving the majority of its rich brand history.
